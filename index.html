Welcome to Blaine's Brass and More

We believe that every musician deserves an instrument with character and history. At Blaine's Brass and More, we meticulously source and offer a refined selection of vintage brass instruments, including trumpets, cornets, mellophones, and flugelhorns. Each instrument in our collection has been chosen for its quality, unique sonic signature, and the stories it holds.

Our Passion

Our mission is to connect musicians with instruments that inspire and elevate their craft. We are dedicated to preserving the heritage of brass instruments and providing a seamless experience for you to find your ideal musical companion. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just beginning your musical journey, we're here to support you.
