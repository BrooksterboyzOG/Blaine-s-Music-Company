Welcome to Blaine's Brass and More

At Blaine's Brass and More, we are passionate about preserving the legacy of vintage brass instruments. Our online collection features a carefully selected range of trumpets, cornets, mellophones, flugelhorns, and more, each with its own unique history and character.

Whether you're a seasoned musician looking for a classic sound, a collector seeking a rare gem, or a student starting your musical journey, we're confident you'll find an instrument that inspires you. Explore our online store to view our current inventory, or visit our "Our Story" page to learn more about our dedication to quality and craftsmanship. Our team is here to connect with you and provide support every step of the way.
