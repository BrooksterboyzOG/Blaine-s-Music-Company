About Blaine's Brass and More

We are passionate about connecting musicians with exceptional vintage brass instruments. Each instrument in our collection is carefully sourced, often restored, and chosen for its unique character and sound. We believe that owning a vintage instrument is not just about playing music; it's about owning a piece of musical history.

Our Promise

We are committed to providing high-quality instruments and excellent customer service. We strive to ensure that every musician finds the perfect instrument to inspire their musical journey. Browse our online store or contact us with any questions – we're here to help you find your voice.
