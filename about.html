Our Story

Discover the passion behind Blaine's Brass and More and our dedication to vintage brass instruments.

Meet Blaine

Hello, I'm Blaine, the founder of Blaine's Brass and More. My journey with brass instruments began many years ago, sparked by a love for their rich history and unique voices. Over time, this passion evolved into a mission: to connect musicians with exceptional vintage instruments that inspire and resonate.

I meticulously source each instrument in our collection, looking for quality craftsmanship, unique sonic character, and a story to tell. Many of our instruments have been lovingly restored to their former glory, ensuring they are not just beautiful to look at but also a joy to play.

Our Mission

At Blaine's Brass and More, our mission is to preserve the heritage of brass instruments by offering a curated selection of vintage pieces to musicians of all levels. We believe that every musician deserves an instrument with character and history, and we are dedicated to providing a seamless and trustworthy experience for you to find your ideal musical companion.

Our Values

  • Passion: We are driven by our love for brass instruments and their history.
  • Quality: We are committed to offering only high-quality, carefully selected instruments.
  • Integrity: We believe in honest and transparent business practices.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your musical journey is important to us, and we are here to support you every step of the way.
  • Preservation: We are dedicated to preserving the legacy of vintage brass instruments for future generations.

Why Choose Us?

When you choose Blaine's Brass and More, you're not just buying an instrument; you're investing in a piece of history. You're joining a community of musicians who appreciate the artistry and craftsmanship of vintage brass. We offer personalized service, expert advice, and a commitment to your satisfaction. We are confident that you will find an instrument in our collection that will inspire you for years to come.
